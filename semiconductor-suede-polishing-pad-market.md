PET Base pads, made from polyester, provide a balance of durability and performance, suitable for various polishing applications. Medium-hard Base pads strike a balance between softness and firmness, allowing for effective material removal while minimizing the risk of damage to sensitive semiconductor surfaces during processing.</p></p>
