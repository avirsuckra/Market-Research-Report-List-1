<p><p>The Contact Linear Position Sensor market is divided into two main types based on output signals: Analog Output Signals and Digital Output Signals. Analog sensors provide continuous voltage or current readings proportional to displacement, allowing for precise measurement in various applications. In contrast, Digital Output Sensors convert positional data into binary signals, providing discrete values which are easier to process in digital systems. Both types cater to different needs in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, influencing design choices and performance specifications.</p></p>
