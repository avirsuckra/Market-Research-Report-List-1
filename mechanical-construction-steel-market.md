<p><p>The mechanical construction steel market primarily includes two types: plain carbon steel (mild steel) and rebar steel. Plain carbon steel is characterized by its low carbon content, offering excellent weldability and ductility, making it ideal for various structural applications. Rebar steel, on the other hand, is specifically designed for reinforced concrete structures, providing tensile strength and durability. Both types are essential for construction activities, ensuring the stability and longevity of buildings and infrastructure.</p></p>
