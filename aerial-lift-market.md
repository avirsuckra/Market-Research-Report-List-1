<p><p>The aerial lift market primarily includes Fixed Grip Chairlifts and Detachable Chairlifts. Fixed Grip Chairlifts feature chairs that are continuously latched to the cable, providing a steady ride but often operating at slower speeds. In contrast, Detachable Chairlifts allow chairs to detach from the cable at the loading and unloading stations, enabling faster transport and enhanced passenger comfort. Both types cater to ski resorts and recreational areas, optimizing user experience in varying terrain and passenger volumes.</p></p>
