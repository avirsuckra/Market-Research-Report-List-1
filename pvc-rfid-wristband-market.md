<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PVC RFID Wristband Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-Frequency (LF)</li><li>High-Frequency (HF)</li><li>Ultrahigh-Frequency (UHF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC RFID wristband market is segmented into three main types based on frequency. Low-Frequency (LF) RFID operates typically at 125-134 kHz, offering short-range reading suitable for access control and animal tracking. High-Frequency (HF) RFID, usually at 13.56 MHz, is widely used for smart tickets and contactless payments due to its moderate range and data transfer speed. Ultrahigh-Frequency (UHF) RFID operates from 300 MHz to 3 GHz, enabling long-range tracking and faster data transfer, ideal for inventory management and event access.</p></p>
