<p><p>The plastic molding additives market comprises several types essential for enhancing polymer performance. Plasticizers improve flexibility and workability, while stabilizers prevent degradation during processing. Flame retardants reduce flammability, ensuring safety in applications. Blowing agents create foam structures for lightweight materials, and nucleating agents promote crystallization, enhancing mechanical properties. Additionally, other additives may include colorants and fillers that further tailor the characteristics of plastics, enabling diverse applications across industries like automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
