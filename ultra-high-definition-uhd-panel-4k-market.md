<p><p>The Ultra High Definition (UHD) panel market, characterized by 4K resolution, comprises three primary technology types: LCD, LED, and OLED. LCD panels utilize liquid crystals for image display, offering good clarity and brightness. LED technology enhances LCDs with backlighting, improving contrast and energy efficiency. OLED panels, on the other hand, feature organic compounds that emit light, providing superior color accuracy and deeper blacks. These technologies cater to diverse consumer preferences, driving innovation and competition in the UHD market.</p></p>
