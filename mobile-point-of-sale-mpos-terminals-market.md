<p><p>The Mobile Point-of-Sale (mPOS) terminals market includes two main types: Non-smart POS and Smart POS. Non-smart POS devices typically consist of basic card readers that connect to mobile devices, offering limited functionality primarily for payment processing. In contrast, Smart POS terminals are equipped with advanced features such as touchscreens, operating systems, and built-in software for inventory management, analytics, and customer engagement. Smart POS systems provide a comprehensive solution for businesses, enhancing overall operational efficiency and customer experience.</p></p>
