<p><p>The electrically-actuated valve market includes several types, each serving distinct applications. Nozzle flapper valves utilize a flapper mechanism to control fluid flow, adjusting based on electrical signals. Jet action valves use a jet stream to create pressure differentials, enabling precise flow control. Dynamic valves are designed for rapid response and adaptation to varying conditions, optimizing performance in dynamic systems. These diverse technologies enhance automation and control in industries such as water treatment, manufacturing, and energy management.</p></p>
