<p><p>The cardiac drug balloon market is categorized into two primary types: cardiac glycosides and non-glycosides. Cardiac glycosides, derived from plant sources, enhance cardiac contractility and regulate heart rhythm, commonly used in heart failure and arrhythmias. In contrast, non-glycosides encompass a broader range of medications, including beta-blockers, calcium channel blockers, and ACE inhibitors, targeting various cardiovascular conditions by managing blood pressure, heart rate, or overall cardiac function. Together, these categories address diverse patient needs in cardiac care.</p></p>
