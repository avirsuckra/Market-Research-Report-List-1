<p><strong>The Automotive Air Intake Resonator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Line Resonators</li><li>Side Branch Resonators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air intake resonator market primarily comprises two types: in-line resonators and side branch resonators. In-line resonators are integrated into the intake system, influencing airflow and reducing noise while maintaining engine performance. Side branch resonators, on the other hand, are attached to the intake path at an angle, utilizing acoustic tuning to dampen specific sound frequencies. Both types are essential for enhancing the acoustic quality of engine noise and improving overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
