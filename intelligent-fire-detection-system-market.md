<p><p>The Intelligent Fire Detection System market encompasses two main types: Addressable Fire Detection Systems and Conventional Fire Detection Systems. Addressable systems utilize advanced technology to connect detectors to a centralized panel, allowing precise location identification of fire sources and faster response times. In contrast, Conventional Fire Detection Systems operate on simpler, zoned structures without specific location identification, making them suitable for smaller facilities. Both systems play crucial roles in enhancing safety and risk management in various environments.</p></p>
