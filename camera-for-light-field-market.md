<p><strong>The Camera for Light Field Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Focused Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Coded Aperture Camera</li><li>Stereo With Plenoptic Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light field camera market features several distinct types. Standard plenoptic cameras capture full light field data using micro-lenses for depth and focus manipulation. Focused plenoptic cameras enhance image sharpness by employing complex lens designs for improved depth fidelity. Coded aperture cameras utilize patterned apertures to gather spatial and directional information, enabling advanced image reconstruction. Stereo with plenoptic cameras combine light field technology with stereo vision, providing enhanced depth perception and 3D imaging capabilities for various applications.</p></p>
