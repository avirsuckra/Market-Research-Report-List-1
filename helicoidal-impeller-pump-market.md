<p><strong>The Helicoidal Impeller Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Screw Impeller Pump</li><li>Closed Screw Impeller Pump</li><li>Semi Open Screw Impeller Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The helicoidal impeller pump market is categorized into three main types: open screw impeller pumps, closed screw impeller pumps, and semi-open screw impeller pumps. Open screw impeller pumps feature exposed blades, making them suitable for handling viscous fluids and solids. Closed screw impeller pumps, with enclosed blades, offer higher efficiency and better pressure capabilities for clean fluids. Semi-open screw impeller pumps blend features of both, allowing for moderate solid handling while maintaining efficiency, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
