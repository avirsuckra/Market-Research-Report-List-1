<p><p>The metal degreaser market is primarily segmented into two types: organic solvents and aqueous cleaners. Organic solvents, typically petroleum-based, offer strong degreasing power for heavy-duty applications, effectively removing oils and greases. However, they may pose environmental and health concerns due to volatile organic compounds. Aqueous cleaners, on the other hand, utilize water-based solutions that are generally safer and more environmentally friendly, making them suitable for lighter cleaning tasks. Overall, the choice between these types depends on application requirements and regulatory considerations.</p></p>
