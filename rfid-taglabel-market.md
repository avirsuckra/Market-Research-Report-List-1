<p><strong>The RFID Tag/Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID tag/label market is divided into two main types: active and passive. Active RFID tags are equipped with their own power source, allowing for a longer read range and continuous data transmission, making them ideal for tracking high-value assets in real-time. In contrast, passive RFID tags do not have an internal power source; they rely on energy from the reader to function, making them more cost-effective and suitable for applications like inventory management and supply chain tracking.</p></p>
