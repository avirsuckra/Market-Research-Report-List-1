<p><p>The anaerobic digestion system market comprises several types, including Upflow Anaerobic Sludge Blanket (UASB), Internal Circulation Reactor (IC Reactor), and Expanded Granular Sludge Bed Digestion (EGSB). UASB systems utilize a blanket of sludge that allows for efficient biogas production. IC Reactors enhance mixing and gas transfer. EGSB systems feature granular sludge that promotes higher loading rates and biogas yield. Other systems may include batch reactors and membrane bioreactors, catering to diverse waste and energy recovery needs.</p></p>
