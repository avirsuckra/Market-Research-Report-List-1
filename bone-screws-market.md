<p><p>The bone screws market can be categorized into three main types: titanium, stainless steel, and bioabsorbable screws. Titanium screws are known for their excellent biocompatibility, strength, and corrosion resistance, making them ideal for permanent fixation. Stainless steel screws are commonly used due to their durability and affordability, although they can cause some tissue irritation. Bioabsorbable screws are an innovative option that gradually dissolve in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal surgeries and promoting natural healing.</p></p>
