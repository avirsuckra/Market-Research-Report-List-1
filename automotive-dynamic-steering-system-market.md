<p><p>The automotive dynamic steering system market primarily includes two main types: hydraulic power steering (HPS) and electric power steering (EPS). HPS uses hydraulic pumps to assist steering efforts, offering a traditional driving feel but with increased energy consumption. Conversely, EPS employs electric motors to adjust steering response, providing better fuel efficiency and customizable driving dynamics. Both systems aim to enhance vehicle handling and driver comfort, with EPS gaining popularity due to its advanced features and energy efficiency.</p></p>
