<p><p>The radial vane damper market comprises three primary types: manual, pneumatic, and electric. Manual type dampers are operated by hand, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for low-demand applications. Pneumatic types utilize compressed air for improved control and response, making them suitable for dynamic environments. Electric dampers leverage electric actuators for precise adjustments and automation, providing enhanced integration with modern control systems. Each type serves distinct needs within HVAC and industrial applications, catering to varying levels of automation and control.</p></p>
