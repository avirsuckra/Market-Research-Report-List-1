<p><strong>The Rapid Diagnostic Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow Test</li><li>Agglutination Assay</li><li>Immunochromatographic Assay</li><li>Immunospot Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Diagnostic Test Kits market comprises several key types. Lateral Flow Tests utilize capillary action to deliver results quickly, often seen in pregnancy and infectious disease tests. Agglutination Assays involve clumping of particles to detect antibodies or antigens, commonly used in blood typing. Immunochromatographic Assays combine principles of immunology and chromatography for rapid results. Immunospot Assays focus on quantifying immune responses by identifying specific spots corresponding to antigen-reactive cells, aiding in various diagnostic and research applications.</p></p>
