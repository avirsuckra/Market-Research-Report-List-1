<p><p>The swimming pool cleaning machine market comprises three main types: robotic pool cleaners, suction pool cleaners, and pressure pool cleaners. Robotic pool cleaners are automated devices that navigate independently, using brushes and filtration systems to effectively clean all surfaces. Suction pool cleaners attach to the pool's skimmer, utilizing the pool's vacuum to remove debris. Pressure pool cleaners operate via water pressure, propelling themselves and collecting debris in a filter bag, providing efficient cleaning for various pool sizes and types.</p></p>
