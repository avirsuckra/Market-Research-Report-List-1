<p><p>The Smart Greenhouse Irrigation System market encompasses various types, including drip irrigation, micro sprinkler irrigation, boom irrigation, and others. Drip irrigation delivers water directly to the plant roots, minimizing waste and enhancing efficiency. Micro sprinkler irrigation disperses water in a fine mist, providing uniform coverage. Boom irrigation uses a mobile arm to distribute water over larger areas, ideal for expansive greenhouse setups. Other irrigation methods may include sub-surface and surface systems, catering to diverse agricultural needs and preferences.</p></p>
