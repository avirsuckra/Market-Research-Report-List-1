In contrast, high-throughput screening allows for the rapid assessment of thousands of samples simultaneously, utilizing automation and advanced technologies. This efficiency enables quicker drug discovery and development processes, catering to the growing demand for efficient and scalable screening methods in pharmaceuticals and biotechnology.</p></p>
