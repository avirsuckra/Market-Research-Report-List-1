<p><strong>The Metallic Paint & Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic paint and coating market is primarily divided into two types: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based metallic paints use organic solvents as their primary medium, offering robust durability and glossy finishes, making them suitable for industrial applications. Conversely, water-based metallic paints utilize water as a solvent, providing lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them more environmentally friendly. While solvent-based coatings excel in toughness, water-based options are favored for residential use due to their safety and ease of application.</p></p>
