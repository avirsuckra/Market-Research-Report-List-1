<p><p>The Rail Flaw Detector market comprises two main types: Double-Track Flaw Detectors and Portable Flaw Detectors. Double-Track Flaw Detectors are designed for simultaneous inspection of both tracks, enhancing efficiency and reducing downtime during maintenance. They are typically mounted on specialized rail inspection vehicles. Portable Flaw Detectors, on the other hand, are handheld or lightweight devices that allow for flexible, on-site inspections in hard-to-reach areas, providing rail maintenance teams with mobile solutions for immediate flaw detection.</p></p>
