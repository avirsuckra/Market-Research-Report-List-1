<p><p>PPLN waveguide chips are essential for nonlinear optical applications, particularly in frequency conversion processes. The market features two primary types: Proton in-diffused (RPE) waveguides, which use proton exchange to create nonlinear optical properties and enhance performance in compact devices, and Ridge-waveguides, which utilize a ridge structure to confine light more efficiently, reducing losses and improving effectiveness in applications like telecommunications and laser technology. Each type addresses specific performance needs in photonic applications.</p></p>
