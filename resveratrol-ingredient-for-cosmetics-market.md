<p><p>Resveratrol in the cosmetics market is derived from three primary sources: synthetic, plant extract, and fermentation. Plant extract resveratrol is naturally sourced from plants like grapes and berries, offering antioxidant benefits and a more holistic appeal. Fermentation-derived resveratrol involves using microorganisms to produce the compound, enhancing potency and bioavailability, and aligning with trends towards sustainable and innovative ingredient sourcing.</p></p>
