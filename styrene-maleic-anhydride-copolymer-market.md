<p><p>The Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer (SMA) market is categorized into two main types: A-SMA and R-SMA. A-SMA, or anhydride-modified SMA, typically exhibits improved adhesive properties and compatibility with various substrates, making it suitable for applications in coatings, adhesives, and sealants. R-SMA, or rubber-modified SMA, incorporates rubber components to enhance flexibility and impact resistance, ideal for automotive and construction applications. Both types leverage the unique properties of SMA for diverse industrial uses, reflecting specific performance needs in the market.</p></p>
