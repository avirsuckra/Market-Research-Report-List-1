<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Cements</li><li>Medium Viscosity Cements</li><li>High Viscosity Vements</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Antibiotic-loaded bone cement is categorized into three viscosity types: low, medium, and high viscosity cements. Low viscosity cements, often used in minimally invasive procedures, offer excellent flowability for precise application. Medium viscosity cements strike a balance between ease of handling and mechanical strength, making them versatile for various orthopedic applications. High viscosity cements provide enhanced mechanical stability and are typically used in load-bearing situations, ensuring effective drug delivery while maintaining structural integrity in orthopedic implants and surgeries.</p></p>
