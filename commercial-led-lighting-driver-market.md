<p><p>The commercial LED lighting driver market encompasses various types, each suited for specific applications. DALI (Digital Addressable Lighting Interface) allows for advanced control and flexibility in lighting systems. 0-10V dimming enables smooth brightness adjustment via a low-voltage control signal. Standard (non-dim) drivers provide consistent lighting without dimming capabilities. Triac dimming is compatible with traditional phase-cut dimmers for smooth transitions. Smart drivers integrate IoT technology for remote management and energy efficiency, enhancing convenience and functionality in commercial settings.</p></p>
